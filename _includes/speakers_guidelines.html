Speakers

Rising Stars Guidelines:

  • Structure: 8 minutes talk followed by 4 minutes Q&#A. Please time your talks beforehand, we have a packed schedule and will enforce limits
  • Day-of Logistics: be prepared to be on-deck during the preceding talk, to allow a speedy and efficient turnaround of 2 minutes
  • Content: keep in mind we will have a broad audience of general roboticists who are not necessarily familiar with your specific discipline
    • Most of your talk should focus on the motivation and impacts of your work, rather than specific technical details
    • Consider answering these questions: What problem does your work aim to solve? Who is affected by these problems? How will your work form a solution?
  • Technical Logistics: bring your own laptop to present slides. We will have a HDMI connection, but you are responsible for bringing any other adapters
