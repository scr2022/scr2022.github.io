Workshops

We are now accepting workshop/tutorial proposals for {{site.conference_title_short}} {{site.year}}. We will be selecting a small number of highly interactive human-centric workshops that highlight real impact. If you are interested in holding a workshop at {{site.conference_title_short}} {{site.year}}, please submit the following:

  • Proposal: A three-page proposal describing the activities of the proposed workshop including:
    1. Title for the workshop
    2. List of organizers and their contact information (indicate primary contact)
    3. Abstract (200 words maximum) summarizing the proposed event
    4. Detailed description of the event highlighting the interactive elements and participant takeaways
    5. Real-world impact and relevance of the workshop
    6. Expected duration (3 hours maximum)
    7. Maximum number of participants that can be supported
    8. Required equipment/infrastructure and logistics
  • Format: IEEE style recommended, PDF format, size 10pt font
  • Submission deadline: July 31th, 2022, Midnight PDT
  • Accepted submissions will be announced on Aug. 15th, 2022, Midnight PDT

Please send your PDF submissions via e-mail to {{site.organizer-email}} with the subject "Workshop Proposal".
