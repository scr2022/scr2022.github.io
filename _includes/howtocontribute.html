How to Contribute

  1. Posters: The main event of this symposium; everyone is invited to apply for a poster. An excellent opportunity to initiate discussions, network, and establish long-lasting collaborations with fellow colleagues across a diverse range of robotic disciplines
  2. Workshops: Hands-on demonstrations or tutorials designed to fully engage the participants in a smaller interactive setting
  3. Rising Stars and New Faculty Spotlights: Late-stage doctoral students, postdocs, and early-stage academics (0-5 years) are invited to apply for a talk with the focus being the human impact of their work
